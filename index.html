Gereon S. Wildermann

Ph.D. Student / Research Assistant

Karlsruhe, Germany

Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)

I am a research assistant at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (Karlsruhe, Germany) in the working group Numerical Modeling and Digitalization for Building Materials and Concrete Structures led by Dr. Ravi Patel. This group is part of the Institute of Concrete Structures and Building Materials, Department of Building Materials, headed by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Frank Dehn.


My research focuses on multi-scale, multi-physics modeling of degradation and corrosion processes in reinforced concrete.
